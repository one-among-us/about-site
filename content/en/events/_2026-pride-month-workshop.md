Jun 30 (Tue) 2-4 pm (Eastern Time) ALL WELCOME, NO RSVP

TV series, anime, video games… Some were just not tailored for us, and hardly offered a safe space for our presence. Sometimes it just hurts, even when we hold many of the memories we've accumulated from childhood, along with our own unique interpretations. We invite you to share these experiences in a safe online space, to talk about the works you have a deep love-hate relationship with, and how they have been criticized, reimagined, and, last but not least, queered, when you're queering the world.
